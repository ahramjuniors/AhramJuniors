ABOUT US

Ahram Juniors is an international youth-led nonprofit club dedicated to forming future leaders and empowering youth by not only helping orphans and providing them with opportunities & supportive environments but also bridging the gender gap in STEAM ( Science, Technology, Engineering, The Arts, and Mathematics).

We foster diversity, inclusivity, and innovation!
